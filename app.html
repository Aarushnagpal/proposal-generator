Core Waiting Periods & Conditions

  1. Initial 30 days waiting period for all medical and life-threatening will be covered after 30 days.
  2. First 24 Months Waiting: Applicable for Specific illnesses and Procedures detailed on Page 2.
  3. Accidental Cover - Hospitalization arising directly out of an ACCIDENT is fully covered from Day-1 of the policy issuance up to sum insured.

Extensive Coverage & Benefits

  • Room Accommodation Eligibility: Any Room is eligible upto sum insured.
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU) Charges: Fully covered up to Sum Insured.
  • Emergency Road Ambulance Charges: Covered up to the Sum Insured on actual basis.
  • Restoration of Sum Insured: Unlimited multiple claims in 1 year up to base sum insured. Triggered even for the same person, for the same illness.
  • GUARANTEED Cumulative Bonus: Increases your sum insured by 100% every year regardless of claims, capped up to 1500%.
  • Pre & Post Hospitalization Expenses: Standard coverage window of 90 Days Pre-Hospitalization & 180 Days Post-Hospitalization.
  • Organ Donor Expenses: Covered up to the Sum Insured limit, including a 30 days pre & post timeline window for each individual case.
  • Listed Non-Medical Items (Disposables): Included & covered up to the base Sum Insured level.
  • Listed Durable Medical Equipments Expense: Covered up to ₹1 Lakh per year limit framework.
  • Advanced Treatments: Fully includes Modern / Advance Treatments, Robotic and Cyberknife Surgeries, and 546 listed Day Care Procedures up to the Sum Insured.
  • Specialized Illness Care: Complete built-in coverage for Mental Illnesses, AYUSH treatments, and HIV, AIDS, or STD medical management.

    Specific 24-Month Illness Exclusions List

    1. Cataract operations.
    2. Hysterectomy for Menorrhagia, Fibromyoma, prolapse of Uterus, or myomectomy for fibroids unless necessitated by malignancy conditions.
    3. Knee Replacement Surgery, Non-infectious Arthritis, Gout, Rheumatism, Osteoarthritis, Osteoporosis, Joint Replacement Surgery, Prolapse of Intervertebral discs, and all Vertebrae Disorders unless caused by an unexpected Accident.
    4. Varicose Veins and Varicose Ulcers treatments.
    5. Stones in the urinary, uro-genital, and biliary systems including calculus diseases and complications.
    6. Benign Prostate Hypertrophy and all types of Hydrocele.
    7. Fissure, Fistula in anus, Piles, all types of Hernia, Pilonidal sinus, Hemorrhoids, and related anal region abscesses.
    8. Chronic Suppurative Otitis Media (CSOM), Deviated Nasal Septum, Sinusitis, Surgery on tonsils / Adenoids, Tympanoplasty, and benign ear, nose, and throat disorders.
    9. Gastric and duodenal ulcers, Cysts, Nodules, Polyps, internal/skin tumors, Breast lumps (unless malignant), and Polycystic Ovarian Diseases.
    10. Any surgery of the genito-urinary system unless directly necessitated by a clear clinical malignancy.
