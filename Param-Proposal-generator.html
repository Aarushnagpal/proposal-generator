Extensive Coverage & Benefits

  • Room Accommodation Eligibility: Any Room is eligible upto sum insured.
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU) Charges: Fully covered up to Sum Insured.
  • Emergency Road Ambulance Charges: Covered up to the Sum Insured on actual basis.
  • Restoration of Sum Insured: Unlimited multiple claims in 1 year up to base sum insured. Triggered even for the same person, for the same illness.
  • GUARANTEED Cumulative Bonus: Increases your sum insured by 100% every year regardless of claims, capped up to 1500%.
  • Pre & Post Hospitalization Expenses: Standard coverage window of 90 Days Pre-Hospitalization & 180 Days Post-Hospitalization.
  • Organ Donor Expenses: Covered up to the Sum Insured limit, including a 30 days pre & post timeline window for each individual case.
  • Listed Non-Medical Items (Disposables): Included & covered up to the base Sum Insured level.
  • Listed Durable Medical Equipments Expense: Covered up to ₹1 Lakh per year limit framework.
  • Advanced Treatments: Fully includes Modern / Advance Treatments, Robotic and Cyberknife Surgeries, and 546 listed Day Care Procedures up to the Sum Insured.
  • Specialized Illness Care: Complete built-in coverage for Mental Illnesses, AYUSH treatments, and HIV, AIDS, or STD medical management.
  • Annual Health Checkup: Organized at company network providers, based on adult member test lists.
